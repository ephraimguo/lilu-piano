About LAIA Piano

Founded in 2007, LAIA Piano now is the largest high-end imported piano distributor in North China, specializing in the wholesale and retail of the highest quality pianos from the world's top brands. Ten years of global management experience has created our most professional team, which enables us to control the quality of every piano from the source and deliver the best piano to every customer's home.

In 2014, Laia Piano completed the whole sales network of Japanese pianos in China, and began the high-end Euro and US piano business. In 2016，we stepped out of China and established a registered company in Japan, starting long-term cooperation with major piano dealers in Europe and the US. In just 5 years, we opened the Japanese high-end piano market and became the chief cooperative supplier of many Japanese piano trading companies.

Now LAIA Piano will officially enter Singapore in 2021. We are committed to let thousands of families in Signapore listen to the world's most beautiful piano sound, feel the world's most luxurious and noble piano temperament. Here you can find the whole Southeast Asia unique variety of extremely rare special models steinway and other European and American top brands. LAIA Piano will be the perfect partner for Singapore's tens of millions of music lovers in their artistic growth path, creating the dreams of all with unparalleled strength.

LAIA Piano History

from 2007 to present, to future

Start from 0

2007 LAIA Piano was founded in Beijing, China. At the beginning, the company mainly engaged in sales and maintenance of Japanese used pianos in Beijing. In the era of underdeveloped Internet marketing, LAIA Piano successfully established itself in Beijing and began to seek new development direction, relying on persistent offline customer visits and strict control of piano quality.

Accumulation and Expansion

After gaining a foothold in Beijing, LAIA Piano officially set up an independent piano acquisition team in Japan in 2009. Gradually start to build standardized processes from acquisition, to shipping, to warehousing, to sales and after-sales. Finally, the company can conveniently control any link, so as to ensure quality while reducing costs, further benefit to the majority of consumers.


Executives of the company began to visit Japan frequently to discuss long-term cooperation with major piano dealers in Japan. Soon, the company was importing a staggering number as 50 to 80 pianos a month. The long-term and stable partnership has made LAIA Piano become the NO.1 VIP of Japanese distributors.

Reach the Top

After completing the original accumulation and standardized process, LAIA Piano grew rapidly. In 2014, the company soon became the largest piano import and export dealer in north China. The sales scope has completely covered the whole China, and there are after-sale partners in all major cities. And now, LAIA Piano has expanded its warehouse to 12,000 square feet, enough to accommodate more than 1,000 pianos.


At the same time, the company formally set up the acquisition team in Frankfurt, Germany and London, England, began to focus on the world's top high-end grand piano. LAIA Piano has won a number of extremely rare models of Steinway, Bösendorfer and Fazioli at London auctions, demonstrated the unparalleled strength of the company.

Step into International

In 2016, LAIA Piano officially registered "莱亜インターナショナル株式会社" in Tokyo, Japan and became a multinational trading company. At the same time, the company's high-end piano acquisition business in Germany, the United Kingdom, the United States has also been fully mature, Steinway inventory has long maintained more than 30 units.


The company's large spot inventory not only meets the retail needs of local customers, but also attracts the interest of customers from other countries around. LAIA Piano's exports grew rapidly and its influence soon spread to Hong Kong, Singapore, Malaysia and other southeast Asian countries.

Explore the Future

In 2021, LAIA Piano set up shop in Singapore, becoming an important foothold for the company in Southeast Asia. Customers in Singapore and its surrounding areas will be able to experience the sound and charm of the world's best pianos up close. LAIA Piano SG will, as always, bring the highest quality, the best price and the most authentic experience to every music lover. In the future, we will work together with every customer to create brilliant future.
